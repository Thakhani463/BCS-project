# BCS-project
Project Title

Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis
Project Overview

This project analyses sales data from a coffee shop business to understand revenue performance, customer behaviour, and product demand. The study focuses on identifying key trends, comparing store performance, and generating actionable insights to support business decision-making.
### Objectives
To analyse overall sales performance across stores
To identify key drivers of revenue
To understand customer purchasing patterns
To provide data-driven recommendations
 ### Data Description
The dataset consists of transactional sales data, including:
Transaction date and time
Product category and product detail
Store location
Quantity sold
Unit price

Revenue was calculated as:
Revenue = Quantity × Unit Price
### Methodology
Data Processing
Data was processed using SQL in Databricks
New variables such as revenue and time buckets were created
Data was grouped by date, store, product, and time
### Data Analysis
Data exported to Excel
Pivot tables used to summarise data
Key metrics analysed:
Revenue
Quantity sold
Store performance
### Data Visualisation

Charts created include:
Monthly revenue trend
Revenue by store
Revenue contribution (pie chart)
Revenue by time of day
Revenue by day of week
Product category performance
Top and bottom products
### Key Insights
Revenue increased significantly from January to June, indicating strong growth
Morning hours generated the highest sales, showing peak customer activity
Revenue was evenly distributed across stores
Coffee and tea products dominated both revenue and quantity sold
A small number of products contributed most of the revenue
Some products showed very low performance and may require review
